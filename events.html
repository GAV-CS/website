About Us

The Gavilan Computer Science Club is available for students to relax, study and collaborate with other students for class or projects involving Computer Science. Our meetings are held every Tuesday, 12-1PM in BUS-118.

Our Goals

Gavilan's CS Club provides networking opportunities both inside and out of the classroom. They also strive to attend major hackathon competitions, as well as provide instructional aides for all members.
